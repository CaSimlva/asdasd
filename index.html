About Us

Building Brasilia's Modern Legacy

Metal Sao Francisco Solucoes em Construcoes e Design Ltda (MSF Solucoes) is a premier construction firm based in the heart of Brasilia, Distrito Federal. Specializing in the nonresidential sector, we bring a focus on durability and modern aesthetics.

Under the leadership of Camila Do Nascimento Silva, our company has established itself as a reliable partner for commercial and industrial projects. We combine the strength of metal construction with sophisticated design principles to create functional, lasting structures.

Whether it involves steel framework, structural upgrades, or complete commercial builds, MSF Solucoes applies rigorous engineering standards to every project.
